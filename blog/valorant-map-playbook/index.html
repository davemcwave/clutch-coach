Valorant Map Playbook

Map knowledge wins eco rounds, forces rotations, and keeps your team calm. Use this playbook to prep any map in fifteen minutes.

Callout Audit

Walk the map in a custom lobby and ping every callout. Rename awkward ones so your stack speaks the same language.

Default + Burst

Write one slow default and one fast hit per site. Define who uses utility first and what triggers the push.

Rotation Rules

Set time-based rules like 'if spike isn't down by 45 seconds, send lurker mid.' Structure stops panic.

Anchor Toolbox

On defense, list two anchor setups per site: utility order, off-angles, and escape paths.

VOD Review

After matches, mark the round and time you misplayed rotations or callouts. Update your playbook weekly so it grows with you.

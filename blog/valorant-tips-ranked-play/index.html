10 Valorant Tips Pros Still Use in Ranked

Here are ten Valorant tips I hammer into every review. They're simple, but when you stack them together you'll feel calmer, sharper, and more helpful to every lobby.

1. Build Two Pistol Plans

Have a scripted pistol round for attack and defense on every map so the team isn't freelancing from the start.

2. Double-Dip Utility

Any time you throw info util, plan the follow-up. Drone? Swing with a flash behind it. Haunt? Reclear with a stun. Don't waste the pressure.

3. Track Rotations

Call when you hear two sets of steps leaving a site. Mid-round info wins more games than early frags.

4. Buy With the Squad

If two teammates save, you save. If two force, you force. Unified econ prevents throwaway rounds.

5. Ping Hitboxes

On vertical maps, ping the exact height you want teammates to clear. It aligns everyone's crosshair placement.

6. Time Mid-Round Utility

Hold one smoke or flash until the clock hits 30 seconds. Rounds are often won by that final delay.

7. Record Bomb Timer Audio

Knowing the exact beep cadence helps you tap or stick with confidence.

8. Pause After Two Losses

Take a one-minute break if you drop two ranked games back-to-back. You'll stop tilt queues.

9. Communicate Intent

When you say, "I'm walking up B, hold my swing," teammates instantly understand their job.

10. Review One Round Immediately

Queue up your replay and study a single round before logging off. Momentum comes from hindsight.

Valorant Ranks Explained

Valorant ranks aren't just colors?they're checklists. Here's what Riot expects at each tier and the plan I give to players stuck on the climb.

Rank Rating Basics

Every competitive game gives or takes rank rating (RR). Win decisively and you earn more RR. Lose with impact and the loss hurts less. Aim for +20 RR average so a bad day doesn't wipe your progress.

Iron to Silver

Master shooter fundamentals: crosshair placement, strafing, and basic utility usage. Drill two default setups per map so you stop dry-pushing.

Gold to Platinum

Communication and trades become the gatekeeper. Record your comms, practice fast rotates, and learn to play off teammates instead of ego-peeking.

Diamond to Ascendant

Utility layering and mid-round calls matter most. Every round should have a plan B. Review losses by asking, 'what info forced us to rotate?'

Immortal and Beyond

Consistency is the differentiator. Track mental fatigue, respect opponents' ult economy, and study your own stats like a coach would.

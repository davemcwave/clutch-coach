Valorant Stats

Valorant Stats That Actually Matter

Dashboard screenshots don't make you better. Focus on four Valorant stats that tell the truth about your gameplay.

Impact Rounds

Log how many rounds you created a numbers advantage (first blood or instant trade). Goal: six or more per game.

Utility Conversion

Track the percent of rounds where your utility led directly to a kill, plant, or space. Support agents should hit forty percent plus.

Economy Discipline

Write down every save versus buy call. If you see three solo buys in a match, tighten communication.

Clutch Attempts

You don't need to win every clutch, but you should convert thirty-five percent of 1v1s. Record why each clutch failed.

How to Log It

Use a simple spreadsheet or Notion template. After each set, screenshot your tracker match and type three bullet points summarizing the stats above.
